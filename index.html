Travel to Magdalen Islands!

The Magdalen Islands form a small archipelago in the Gulf of Saint Lawrence with a land area of 205.53 square kilometres. Though closer to Prince Edward Island and Nova Scotia, the islands form part of the Canadian province of Quebec.

I have been to this amazing destination twice and truly is worth it! Here is what you can't miss:

  • Lobsters
  • Walk on the beach
  • Fishing
  • Kite Surf

Here are the different ways to get there:

  1. Plane
  2. Boat
  3. Car, and lift from ferry

Now book your tickets and enjoy!
